Latitude vs. Cloudiness

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator. More interestling, however, is the fact that the sourthern hemishpere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemishpere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
